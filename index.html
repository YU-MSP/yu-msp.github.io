The MSP is working towards the research and development for audio data, video stream, and 3D meshes, as well as still image.

ain research topics include digital watermarking, JPEG/JPEG2000 standard, data compression, 3D graphics, and quality measurement for transmitted multimedia data. Now, there are three members of doctor course, and they are performing two research projects, “Joint Watermarking and Compression in 3-D Irregular Meshes via Multi-resolution Wavelet Analysis” (KOSEF, Korea and CNRS, France) and “A Study on the QoS-guaranteed Traffic Engineering and Multimedia Service Platform in the Next Generation Wired/Wireless Integrated Networking Environment” (MIC, Korea). The alumni are active in national research institutes and telecommunication company.
