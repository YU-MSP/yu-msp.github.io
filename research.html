Research Topic
  • Main research topics have included digital watermarking, JPEG/JPEG2000 standard, data compression, 3D graphics, and wavelet transform.
  • Recent researches focus on CNN(Convolutional Neural Network) based image processing and computer vision such as Camera sensor ISP(Image Signal Process), moving object detection on dynamic scene, traffic sign/light recognition, BSD(Blind Spot Detection), visibility enhancement of HUD(Head-Up Display) for ADAS(advanced driving assistance systems) and self-driving car.
  • Non-linear control theory is also interested research area.
